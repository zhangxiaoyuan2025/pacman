How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Use Arrow Keys or WASD to move Pac-Man
  • Eat all dots to complete the level
  • Avoid ghosts unless you have a Power Pellet
  • Collect fruits for bonus points

Scoring System

  • Small Dots: 10 points
  • Power Pellets: 50 points
  • Ghost (vulnerable): 200-1600 points
  • Fruit Bonus: 100-5000 points

The Legacy of Pac-Man

First released in Japan on May 22, 1980, Pac-Man revolutionized the gaming industry and became one of the most successful arcade games of all time. Created by Toru Iwatani, the game's simple yet addictive gameplay has entertained generations of players.

The 30th Anniversary edition celebrates this incredible legacy while bringing the classic gameplay to modern platforms. Experience the same thrilling chase, strategic gameplay, and iconic sound effects that made Pac-Man a cultural phenomenon.
